# Build Journal – Project Byteboard (Custom Mechanical Keyboard)

---

## April 25, 2025
Began planning for a fully custom mechanical keyboard inspired by Peng Zhihui's "HelloWord" keyboard. My goal: to build a compact, modular, and aesthetic board tailored for both programming and creative work.

## April 27, 2025
Analyzed existing open-source designs. Decided to go for a hot-swappable PCB with an OLED and encoder for enhanced UX.

## May 1, 2025
Identified required components (switches, diodes, MCU, OLED, encoder, etc.). Created a tentative bill of materials (BOM) and sourcing list.

## May 8, 2025
Prototyped the case in CAD software based on switch spacing and key layout. Designed it with aesthetic and ergonomic considerations, including support for a brass encoder knob.

## May 18, 2025
Completed all KiCad validation. Gerbers and BOM are ready for manufacturing. Preparing to submit files to PCB fabrication and begin sourcing components.

## What’s Next?
Seeking funding support to purchase components, order the PCB, and manufacture the case.
